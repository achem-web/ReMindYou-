If you are experiencing mental health concerns, please contact:

National Center for Mental Health (NCMH) Crisis Hotline
Call: 0917-899-USAP (8727) or 1553 (landline)
Available 24/7 for emotional support and crisis intervention.

Hopeline PH
Call or text: 0917-558-HOPE (4673) or 2919 (Globe & TM subscribers only)
Available 24/7 for anyone in need of mental health support.

Bantay Bata 163 (Children and Youth Support)
Call: 163 (Hotline)
They also provide services for children and families in crisis situations.
